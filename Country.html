Flag, Emblem, History

Flag of the UK — The UK flag is a mixture of the Northern Irish, Scottish and English flags.

State emblem

The inscription on the state emblem of the UK reads: God and my right.

The history of the United Kingdom begins with the formation of the Kingdom of Great Britain in 1707 following the unification of England and Scotland. In 1801, it became the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland following the union with Ireland. In 1922, following the War of Independence, six regions of Northern Ireland remained within the UK, leading to the country's renaming in 1927 to the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland.
