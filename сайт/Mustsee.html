Must-See Places in UK

Ben Nevis

The highest mountain in Great Britain, located in Scotland at 1,344 meters, and a Scottish distillery founded in 1825 at its foot. The mountain is known for its challenging routes and beautiful views.

Snowdonia

a national park and mountain region in north Wales, renowned for its spectacular scenery, including Britain's highest mountain, Mount Snowdon (1085m), as well as valleys, lakes and coastline.

River Thames

The Thames is one of the most famous rivers in Great Britain, flowing through London and one of England's longest rivers. The river is of great economic and shipping importance, and it is the river most often associated with London. The Thames Estuary forms a vast estuary that extends into the North Sea and is home to London's major ports.

Lake District

a picturesque mountainous region in northwest England, in the county of Cumbria, famous for its numerous lakes and mountains, including England's highest point, Scafell Pike.

Loch Ness

a large, deep Scottish lake, famous for its beauty and legends about the Loch Ness Monster (Nessie), and the Loch Ness blackberry – a popular thornless variety, distinguished by high productivity, large sweet berries and good transportability.
